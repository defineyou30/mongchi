## Generation Direction

Built-in Imagegen produced four text-free pixel-gloss garden backdrops. Each
used the approved Mongchi garden palette and a feature-specific prop family:
camera and polaroids for creation, letters and speech charms for chat,
scrapbook finds for memories, and portrait cards and ribbons for poses. All
marketing copy, device frames, and real app UI were composited afterward with
deterministic raster tooling.
